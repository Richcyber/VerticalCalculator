Description

This app shows you the basic mathematical operations diagram (addition, subtraction, multiplication and division) with vertical method.

  • Easy to use (calculator-like interface).
  • Support addition, subtraction, multiplication and division operations.
  • Support decimal point.
  • The result page supports gesture zoom for easy viewing.
  • Detect repeating decimal in division operation, and display repetend when the length of decimal part less than 30 digits.
